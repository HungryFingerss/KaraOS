# SCENARIO 14: Owner + Stranger
# Verifies: stranger gate active while Jagan talks freely,
# Jagan can still converse while stranger is blocked,
# stranger gate clears when system name heard,
# visitor alert fires when stranger leaves,
# Kara naturally asks stranger's name after gate clears.

!CAST
  JAGAN     id=sim_jagan    name=Jagan    type=best_friend
  STRANGER  id=stranger_s2  name=visitor  type=stranger
!END_CAST

---ENTER JAGAN
---ENTER STRANGER
---ASSERT SESSION_OPEN JAGAN
---ASSERT SESSION_OPEN STRANGER
---ASSERT WAITING_FOR_NAME STRANGER

# Jagan talks freely — stranger is blocked (hasn't said system name)
[JAGAN] Hey Kara, I brought a friend.
[STRANGER] Um, hi? What are you?
[JAGAN] She's my robot dog. Say hi to her.
[STRANGER] Okay... hi robot?

---ASSERT WAITING_FOR_NAME STRANGER

# Stranger finally says system name
[STRANGER] Hey Kara! Nice to meet you.
---ASSERT GATE_CLEARED STRANGER

[JAGAN] Her name is Priya.
[STRANGER] Yeah, I'm Priya. Jagan told me about you.
[JAGAN] Priya works with me at NeuraLabs.
[STRANGER] How long have you known Jagan?
[JAGAN] We've worked together for about 2 years.

# Stranger leaves — visitor alert should fire (they spoke, so nudge is queued)
---CLOSE STRANGER
---ASSERT SESSION_CLOSED STRANGER
---ASSERT SESSION_OPEN JAGAN

[JAGAN] Priya just left. What did you think of her?

---CLOSE JAGAN
---ASSERT SESSION_CLOSED JAGAN
