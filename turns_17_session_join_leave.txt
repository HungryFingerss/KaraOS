# SCENARIO 17: Person enters and leaves mid-conversation
# Verifies: new_face flag propagates on ENTER (Kara notices Kavya arrive),
# room context updates when Kavya leaves,
# LEAVE stops heartbeat for Kavya,
# Jagan's session stays clean throughout.

!CAST
  JAGAN  id=sim_jagan  name=Jagan  type=best_friend
  KAVYA  id=sim_kavya  name=Kavya  type=known
!END_CAST

---ENTER JAGAN
---ASSERT SESSION_OPEN JAGAN

[JAGAN] Hey Kara.
[JAGAN] How are you doing today?
[JAGAN] I wanted to ask you something about AI development.
[JAGAN] What do you think is the most important thing to get right in robotics?

# Kavya walks in mid-conversation — Kara should notice (new_face flag)
---ENTER KAVYA
---ASSERT SESSION_OPEN KAVYA

[JAGAN] Oh hey Kavya, you made it.
[KAVYA] Hi Jagan! Hey Kara, nice to finally meet you in person.
[JAGAN] Kavya's been wanting to meet you for a while.
[KAVYA] Jagan has told me so much about this project.
[KAVYA] Can you remember who I am?
[JAGAN] She knows about you actually. I've mentioned you.

# Kavya leaves
---LEAVE KAVYA
---CLOSE KAVYA
---ASSERT SESSION_CLOSED KAVYA
---ASSERT SESSION_OPEN JAGAN

[JAGAN] Kavya had to run. Just us again.
[JAGAN] Did you recognize her?

---CLOSE JAGAN
