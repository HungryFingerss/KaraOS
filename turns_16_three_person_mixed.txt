# SCENARIO 16: Owner + Known + Stranger (3-person mixed room)
# Verifies: room context with 3 active sessions,
# correct role labels (best_friend / known / visitor),
# stranger gate works in a full room,
# each person's memory remains isolated,
# Kara handles being addressed by different people mid-conversation.

!CAST
  JAGAN     id=sim_jagan    name=Jagan    type=best_friend
  KAVYA     id=sim_kavya    name=Kavya    type=known
  STRANGER  id=stranger_s5  name=visitor  type=stranger
!END_CAST

---ENTER JAGAN
---ENTER KAVYA
---ENTER STRANGER
---ASSERT SESSION_OPEN JAGAN
---ASSERT SESSION_OPEN KAVYA
---ASSERT SESSION_OPEN STRANGER
---ASSERT WAITING_FOR_NAME STRANGER

[JAGAN] Hey Kara, we've got a full house today.
[KAVYA] Hi Kara! It's been a while.
[STRANGER] Um hello? What is this thing?
[JAGAN] Say her name and she'll talk to you.
[STRANGER] Oh — hey Kara!
---ASSERT GATE_CLEARED STRANGER

[KAVYA] Kara, this is my friend Ananya.
[STRANGER] Yeah, hi Kara. I'm Ananya.
[JAGAN] Ananya, Kavya, and I all work together.
[KAVYA] What do you know about Jagan, Kara?
[STRANGER] Do you remember everyone you meet?
[JAGAN] She has a pretty good memory.
[KAVYA] Kara, do you know who I am?
[STRANGER] This is kind of amazing actually.

---CLOSE STRANGER
---ASSERT SESSION_CLOSED STRANGER
---ASSERT SESSION_OPEN JAGAN
---ASSERT SESSION_OPEN KAVYA

[JAGAN] Ananya left. Just the three of us now — wait, two of us.
[KAVYA] Yeah she had to go. Kara, what did you think?

---CLOSE KAVYA
---CLOSE JAGAN
